HR LEAVE APPROVAL WORKFLOW:

Employee Flow:
  1. Click "Apply Leave" on dashboard
  2. Fill in leave type, dates, reason
  3. Submit request
  4. ✅ Success message
  5. 📧 HR receives email notification
  6. View request status in "My Leave Requests"

HR Manager Flow:
  1. 📧 Receive email notification
  2. Go to Leave page
  3. Click "Pending Requests" tab
  4. View requests in card format
  5. Click "✅ Approve" or "❌ Reject"
  6. 📧 Employee receives notification
  7. View all requests in "All Requests" tab

Email Notifications:
  • Leave Application → HR (with employee details)
  • Approval → Employee (with leave details)
  • Rejection → Employee (with reason)

Status Badges:
  • ⏳ Pending (Yellow) - Awaiting approval
  • ✅ Approved (Green) - Approved by HR
  • ❌ Rejected (Red) - Rejected by HR
